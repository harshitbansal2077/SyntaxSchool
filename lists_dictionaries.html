Introduction

This tutorial introduces lists and dictionaries, two important data structures in Python.

Lists

Lists are ordered collections of items. Here’s how to create a list:

fruits = ["apple", "banana", "cherry"]

Dictionaries

Dictionaries are collections of key-value pairs. Here’s how to create a dictionary:

person = {"name": "John", "age": 30}
